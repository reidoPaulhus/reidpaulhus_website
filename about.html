About

My name is Reid and I am a generalist programmer working in game development. I have a B.Sc. in Computer Science with a minor in New Media from the University of Lethbridge.

What drives me most is the joy of bringing ideas to life through code — whether it's creating immersive worlds, solving complex technical puzzles, or innovating gameplay mechanics that captivate players. The thrill of seeing players enjoy and connect with the games I've helped create is what fuels my passion and motivates me to continually push the boundaries of what's possible in game development.

I have several years of experience primarily programming in C++, and have focused on using Unreal Engine.
